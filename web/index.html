No — and that's intentional. Weekly tracking gives you trend data that actually matters: how your visibility changes over time, what moved, and why. Real-time noise doesn't help you make decisions.

Nothing. You sign up, tell us your brand and prompts, and we handle everything. No tracking pixels, no API keys, no DNS records. Your report arrives every Monday.

We generate a starter prompt set based on your industry and brand during onboarding. You can edit, add, or remove prompts anytime — and we'll suggest new ones as your category evolves.
